CHEESE PLATTERS

CHEESE: A wonderful gift for any occasion!


Order a platter and send it to your friends and family with our FREE delivery service across downtown Toronto! Our platters come in three different sizes and include an assortment of delicous hard and soft cheeses, charcuterie, and crackers. Select your platter size below!
