HISTORY OF
CHEESE MAGIC

FINELY SLICING SINCE 1987

Just over three decades ago, there was a small, narrow cheese shop located at 149 Baldwin Street (where Jerk Spot is today). Ping Chiu, a film studies graduate of the University of Windsor took over the cheese store from an Italian couple who had enough of all their frommagio. This then 20-odd-year-old entrepreneur from Singapore named the shop Cheese Magic, and for the past 26 years, it has been located at its current location at 182 Baldwin Street. Thanks to the support of customers, it continues to be one of Kensington Market's narrowest and most potent smelling stores today!

Ping takes pride in the quality of her cheese and appreciates the business, support, and friendship of her customers :)
