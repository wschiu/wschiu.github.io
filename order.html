CHEESE PICK-UP

To place an order for pick up, please follow these simple steps below (4 items minimum):


1. Look through our large menu of cheese and other products on THIS PAGE
2. Send an email with the subject line "Cheese Magic Pick Up Order" to Ping at cheesemagic@msn.com with your name and order (list of cheeses with their quantities).
3. Please note the pick up date and time. Kindly ensure to provide at least one day's notice and choose a time during our store hours.
4. We will have your order ready for the day and time you selected. Payments can be made in store with cash or card.
