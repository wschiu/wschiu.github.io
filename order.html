CHEESE DELIVERIES & PICK-UP

To do our part in helping to stop the spread of COVID-19, we are offering pick-ups and making FREE deliveries across downtown Toronto so that you can stay home and still enjoy your favourite cheeses!


FOLLOW THESE SIMPLE STEPS TO ARRANGE FOR PICK-UP OR HAVE CHEESE DELIVERED RIGHT TO YOUR DOORSTEP:


1. Look through our large menu of cheese and other products on THIS PAGE
2. Fill out the order form below and select if you would like to pick-up in store or delivery
3. Pick-Ups: You can pay in store using cash, debit, or credit (tap available)

Delivery: You will receive an invoice via email when your order is ready, which you can pay online using credit, debit, or PayPal
4. Pick-Ups: We will have your order ready for the day and time you selected (please ensure it is during store hours and with one day notice)

Delivery: We will deliver your groceries on the following available delivery date

DELIVERIES

There is a $25 minimum order for deliveries.

Our delivery area spans west to Dufferin, north to Dupont, east to Parliament, and south to Lake Shore Blvd (see map).

Once we receive your order, we will place it in line for the next available delivery date (normally within 2 days).

There is NO delivery fee, but tips for the delivery person are always greatly appreciated :)

If you have any questions, please contact orders@cheesemagic.ca.
